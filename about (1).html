Since 2014

A Garage Roaster That Grew Into a Gathering Place

Artisan began with a secondhand roaster, forty pounds of green beans, and a simple idea: coffee tastes better when someone actually cares who's drinking it. Our founders roasted their first batches in a garage, delivering bags by bike to friends who kept asking for more.

A decade later, the roaster is bigger, the shop has real walls, and the bike has been retired — but the standard hasn't moved an inch. Every bean is still tasted before it's bought, and every cup is still made like it's for a friend.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Why It Works

Our Mission

To roast honest, traceable coffee and serve it in a space where people feel welcome to slow down, whether they're here for five minutes or five hours.

Our Vision

A neighborhood where good coffee is inseparable from good relationships — with the farmers who grow it, the baristas who pour it, and the regulars who return for it.

Why Customers Love Us

Consistency they can set their watch to, baristas who remember their names, and beans that taste like someone actually chose them on purpose.

Our Promise

If a cup isn't right, we'll remake it — no questions, no awkwardness. Your morning matters more than our margin.

From Farm to Cup

Our Coffee Roasting Process

Five steps, followed the same way every single batch.

1

Sourcing

Green beans bought direct from partner farms we've visited ourselves.

2

Cupping

Every lot is tasted and scored before it earns a spot in our roaster.

3

Roasting

Small batches, roasted by hand to bring out each bean's true character.

4

Resting

Beans rest 24–72 hours to let carbon dioxide escape and flavor settle.

5

Brewing

Ground to order and brewed within minutes of you placing it.
